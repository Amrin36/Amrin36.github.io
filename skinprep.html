Skin Prep

Preparing your skin for makeup ensures that it goes on evenly and stays put. The better your skin looks to begin with, the better the makeup will look. Therefore, take steps to cleanse your skin,apply moisturizer and prime the skin before beginning any makeup application.

If you haven’t been using makeup primer, then you’re really missing out. Whether you wear a full face of makeup everyday or you prefer to keep it simple with BB cream and tinted lip balm, makeup primer is a godsend when trying to combat shine or create a smooth base for foundation.

Makeup primer is a base for foundation or face makeup that allows it to go on smoother and last longer.
